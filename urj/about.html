Urjotsav - 2K24

Its been 15 years since the start of PIEMR Urjotsav, and with each passing year, the excitement surrounding the event has grown multifold. The highly anticipated TechFest has returned, generating even greater excitement and enthusiasm this time. Urjotsav provides students with a platform to develop and showcase their scientific and practical problem-solving skills, nurturing their talent and creativity. The Fest includes workshops, competitions, and Tech Talks to guide and inspire young minds. Urjotsav provides an outstanding opportunity for students to develop their skill sets and lay the foundation for a promising future.
