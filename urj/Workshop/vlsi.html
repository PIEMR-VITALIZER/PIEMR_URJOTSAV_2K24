Very Large Scale Integration

The ever-growing complexity of Artificial Intelligence (AI) models demands ever-increasing processing power. Here, we explore the critical role of Very Large Scale Integration (VLSI) in designing chips specifically optimized for running AI algorithms efficiently. By cramming billions of transistors onto a single chip, VLSI paves the way for faster, more powerful, and energy-saving AI applications.
